Our Privacy Policy



Privacy Policy effective as of June 22, 2017.

Please note that our privacy policy applies to our website, civicusengineering.github.io. This website contains links to other websites, once you leave our website, you will no longer be subjected to our privacy policy.

What is a privacy policy?

Privacy policy is a statement or legal document in which the company discloses what information is collected and how it is being used. Privacy policies are legally required to be present on websites to inform the users and protect their privacy.

We at Civicus Engineering respect all user’s privacy.

Definitions

  1. Users are anyone who visits our website and messages us.
  2. Job applicants are users that are interested in a job opening and have sent their information through the job opening form.
  3. Personal information is your name, email, address, IP address, birthdate, etc. It is any information that can be used to personally identify a user or job applicant.

What information do we collect? Why?

We collect any and all information you give us with consent such as:

Name, Email, Resumes, CV
Information sent through the contact form will be collected. This allows us to communicate with users with any inquiries they have. Information sent through the opening positions form will be collected. This allows us to maintain a relationship between the job applicant and the company with business inquiries and hiring opportunities. We keep track of those who have applied for a position. Resumes and/or cover letters will be kept for hiring purposes and records.
IP Address
IP addresses will be collected as evidence and protection against abuse of messaging system. Abuse may include advertisement, harrasment, etc. IP Addresses are addressed in our Terms of Use.
Cookies
Cookies are a small text file created by a website that is stored in the user's computer. Cookies provide a way for the website to recognize you and keep track of your preferences. We use cookies to track the user’s actions when navigating our site. We use this information to optimize our website to enhance and improve user experience.



We are an engineering company committed to providing excellent service to our clients. We hire the most suitable employees for the company and position. Information sent to us will ONLY be between the company and the job applicants. No third parties are involved.



By sending us your information, you acknowledge that you have read and agree with the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
