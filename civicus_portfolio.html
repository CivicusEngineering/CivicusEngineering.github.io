Current Projects



Hydrology Report of Proposed Site in Irvine, Orange County

Civicus Engineering was hired to analyze the existing conditions of the proposed site in Irvine to design a detention basin at the foot of the local watershed. The client was a Cal Poly Pomona hydrology professor of CE451.

Soil Report for Proposed College of Agriculture Building

Civicus Engineering was hired to analyze the soil present at the site. The site is located on campus of Cal Poly Pomona, near the orange orchards at the end of Citrus Lane. Disturbed and undisturbed soil samples were obtained in order to determine their characteristics. Sieve Analysis, consolidation test, compaction test, hydrometer test, and soil classification were conducted to determine the suitable foundation for the structure. The client was Cal Poly Pomona (CE327L Geotech Lab).
