Structural Division


Our Structural Division consists of structural engineering and architecture. This division provides services to government contracts, business clients, and residential clients.

Our goal is to design structures based on green design - with reusability and sustainability in mind. Our structural engineers will design an efficient framework of the structures while our architects mask the ugly aspects of the structure.

    Our services include:

  • Computer Aided Design (CAD) - 2D and 3D
  • Building Information Modeling (BIM)
  • Grading Plans
  • Design
  • Structural Investigation
  • Structural Analysis
  • Architecture
