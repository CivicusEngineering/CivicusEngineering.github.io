Who Are We?

We are a civil engineering and construction company that specializes in geotechnical, surveying, structural, and construction services. Our goal is to provide an efficient and seamless construction process.

This company started in October of 2016. While in a geotechnical engineering class,our current CEO came up with the idea of pretending to be a company for an assigned project. The project was a soil report. Our CEO created a fictional company that would write the report, just to make the project more interesting and thus...a company was born.
