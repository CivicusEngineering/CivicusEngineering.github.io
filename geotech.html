Geotechnical Division


Our Geotechnical Division is the most "boring" division in the company. We study and analyze the soil of a proposed site and design foundations. This division provides services to government contracts, business clients, and residential clients.

    Our services include:

  • Sieve Analysis
  • Direct Shear Tests
  • Consolidation Test
  • Hydrometer Analysis
  • Proctor Compaction Test
  • Geologic Studies
  • Foundation Design
  • Boring
  • Drainage
